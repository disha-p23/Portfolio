About Me

Building AI that matters

I'm a final-year B.Tech student at GITAM University (Hyderabad) specializing in AI & ML. I don't just study machine learning — I build, ship, and measure it. From anomaly detectors and image classifiers to end-to-end deployed pipelines, my work sits at the intersection of research and engineering.

Currently interning at TCS as an AI Engineer, building a multimodal document intelligence system for Jupiter Hospital — combining NLP, speech synthesis, and OCR to make patient information more accessible.

I also research why LLMs hallucinate and lie to please users (sycophancy), and explore how federated learning can enable privacy-preserving AI in sensitive domains.
